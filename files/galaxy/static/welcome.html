The Czech Galaxy instance

Welcome to the public Galaxy instance of E-Infra CZ and ELIXIR CZECH REPUBLIC hosted at MetaCentrum to enable accessible, reproducible and transparent computational research. Galaxy supports thousands of documented and maintained tools that are free to use.




Come join us at the Galaxy Community Conference 2024!


Please visit UseGalaxy CZ Documentation before using the service. If this is your first time using Galaxy platform, we strongly recommend taking an interactive tour which is enabled at the bottom of the page. In case of any further questions, please contact us at regalaxy@rt.cesnet.cz.



About the Galaxy platform


Galaxy is an open platform for supporting data intensive research. Galaxy is developed by The Galaxy Team with the support of many contributors.

The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NHGRI, NSF, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins University.
